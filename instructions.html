INSTRUCTIONS

  • Harry is on the adventure to destroy the seven Horcruxes. Help him to destroy them to kill the Voldemort.
  • You will be dealing with 7 levels of magic in your journey.
  • In each level you will face a mystery solving which will help you and Harry to destroy a Horcrux at every level.
  • Solving each level will open you another mystery.
  • Levels 1-2 include preliminary magic, 3-5 mediocre magic and from level 6-7 you will be dealing with extraordinary magic.
  • Only two attempts will be provided for each level. Second attempt will reduce the score to half.
  • 45 minutes will be alloted to destroy the first five horcruxes. And for the next 45 minutes you will be redirected to the hackerearth contest to destroy the next two horcruxes.
